Domains

The list of available domains is presented on the Domains&Tools page. In order to display only domains, you need to set the Domain filter.
The use of domains can be:
  • Free
  • Paid with a free period
  • Paid
To view purchased and paid domains, you need on this page:
  • Login
  • Enable filter Only accessed
To connect any of the domains directly to the workspace, select add.
